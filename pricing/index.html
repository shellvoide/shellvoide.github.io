FAQ

The details.

What is included in every KLUE plan?

Real-browser exploit validation, working proof, branded PDF and JSON reports, all compliance frameworks, and a free retest after you fix.

How does billing work?

KLUE plans are billed monthly in USD and you can change or cancel anytime. Enterprise agreements can be billed annually with custom terms and pay-as-you-go top-ups.

What is the difference between plans and services?

Plans are self-serve KLUE subscriptions you run yourself, on your own cadence. Services are hands-on engagements delivered by our team and quoted per scope.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. Move between Starter, Pro, and Enterprise whenever your surface or volume changes. Enterprise unlocks custom quotas, longer runs, and selectable concurrency.

Do you offer one-off engagements?

Absolutely. Pentests, red team operations, and audits can be booked as standalone engagements with no subscription required.

How is KLUE different from a scanner?

KLUE reasons about your systems instead of just matching known patterns, so it finds logic flaws and chains them into real attack paths, then proves each one with a working exploit.
